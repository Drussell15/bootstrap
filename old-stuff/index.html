About Me

Dale currently teaches High School English and is transitioning to a software development career. While teaching in Arizona, Florida and Ohio he has earned a Master's in Library and Information Science from Wayne State University. His academic interests focoused on open educational resources, open access of Information and open source technologies used in information sharing (like digital repository platform Islandora). Dale is currently completing the full-stack web development bootcamp at the University of Arizona.
